Focus — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 3, 2026

Focus (the “App”) is a task and focus-timer app operated by Doris Legacy AI, LLC (“we,” “us”). This policy explains what data we collect, why, who processes it, and the choices you have — including how to delete your account and all of your data from within the App.

We do not use your data for advertising, and we do not track you across other companies’ apps or websites.

1. Data we collect

We collect only what the App needs to work. Every item below is linked to your account and is used solely to operate the App’s features — never for tracking.

Task time estimates you enter may be aggregated anonymously with other users’ estimates to improve AI time suggestions. Aggregated estimates are not tied to your identity.

2. How your data is used and stored

  • On your device. Your tasks, points, and profile photo are stored locally on your device.
  • Leaderboard (Apple iCloud/CloudKit). Your public profile — display name, photo, points, streak, and activity counts — is stored in Apple’s CloudKit public database so it can appear on the global leaderboard visible to other users.
  • AI time suggestions (Google via Firebase). When you add a task, the task title (and any anonymous community estimates) is sent through Firebase AI Logic to Google’s Gemini model to suggest a realistic duration. No API key or account credential is sent.

3. Third-party services

We rely on the following processors, each governed by its own privacy policy:

  • Apple — Sign in with Apple, iCloud/CloudKit sync and leaderboard, in-app subscriptions. See Apple’s Privacy Policy.
  • Google / Firebase — Google Sign-In, Firebase App Check, and Firebase AI Logic (Gemini) for time suggestions. See Firebase Privacy and Google’s Privacy Policy.

We do not control and are not responsible for these third-party services’ own data practices.

4. Community content, reporting & blocking

The leaderboard shows other users’ display names and photos. You agree not to use an offensive display name or photo. From any profile you can report or block another user; blocked users are hidden from you, and reported profiles are reviewed. We may remove profiles or terminate accounts that violate these rules.

5. Deleting your account and data

You can permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time, directly in the App: Profile & Account → Delete Account. This removes your public leaderboard profile and erases your local tasks, points, and profile photo. The action cannot be undone.

You may also request deletion by contacting us at the address below.

6. Children

Focus is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us personal information, contact us and we will delete it.

7. Data security

Data in transit is protected with standard HTTPS/TLS encryption. Access to backend data relies on Apple and Google’s platform security. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by the “Last updated” date above and, where appropriate, noted in the App.
